The Problem

AI tools are powerful.
But painfully fragmented.

Every time you switch models, you lose everything. You re-explain. You repeat. You forget what worked.

🔄

Context Vanishes

Switch from Claude to ChatGPT and your entire conversation history, preferences, and past decisions disappear instantly.

🔁

Endless Repetition

You write the same project background, constraints, and style preferences over and over - for every new chat, every new tool.

🧩

Fragmented Tools

Notes in Notion, chats in ChatGPT, docs in Drive, code in Cursor - your knowledge is scattered across different apps.

🫧

AI Hallucinations

Without your history as grounding, AI models confidently suggest solutions you already tried and rejected last week.

🔍

Lost Insights

The Solution

Everything AI, unified.

A single elegant workspace where your AI conversations and personal knowledge weave together and persist forever.

01 - CORE FEATURE

Unified Memory Engine

An AI agent automatically summarizes every chat into structured, editable memory entries - key decisions, paths tried, preferences, constraints. When you switch models, your entire context travels with you instantly. Zero repetition.

02 - MODEL LAYER

Seamless Model Switching + Fusion

Access multiple AI models in one interface. Switch mid-conversation without losing a beat. Or activate Fusion Mode — blend outputs from multiple models simultaneously for answers that outperform any single AI.

03 - KNOWLEDGE

Universal Capture

Drop in a YouTube link, PDF, article, or recording. Weavim transcribes, extracts key insights and decisions, and stores them directly in your Unified Memory as editable entries - ready to inject into any future AI chat.

04 - WORKSPACE

Visual Canvas Workspace

An infinite drag-and-drop canvas to organize memory cards, files, and ideas spatially. Chat with any AI model while the full canvas context is automatically injected into every prompt.
